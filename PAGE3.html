WHAT WE DO

REAL ESTATE

We at roel construction and real estate companies have a symbiotic relationship, often working together on projects. Real estate firms often start development projects, which involve acquiring land or properties to build new buildings or improve existing ones. They then typically hire construction companies to execute the construction work.

DEMOLITION

Before starting demolition work, construction companies will study all available documents and drawings, and perform a survey of the building, structure, or services to be demolished. This may include recording any unrestrained works or services, or existing damages. The demolition process typically involves the following phases: Surveying the building and surrounding area, Removing hazardous materials, Creating demolition plans, and Safety measures for everyone working on the project.

CONSULTING AND DESIGN

Construction companies use consultants to help with design and planning, including structural design, cost estimation, and project management. Consultants may also help with: Business assistance: How to set up projects, and general technical advice Worker safety: Noting any environmental issues at the site Contract negotiations: Drawing up and administering contract negotiations between the contractors and construction firm Quality control: Ensuring that contract specifications and schedules are met

COMMERCIAL PLUMBERING

Commercial plumbing is important for keeping commercial buildings safe and operational. It helps companies and organizations run efficiently, adhere to rules, and offer a sanitary atmosphere for staff and clients. Commercial plumbing can include: Clogged drains, Leaking or burst waterworks, Heated water tanks, Installing new piping, and Connecting to the municipal water mains. For a commercial project, it's best to partner with a reputable plumbing services provider. Commercial plumbers are more than just maintenance people, they are master tradesmen who have to understand and deliver a high level of customer service because it impacts customers immediately.
